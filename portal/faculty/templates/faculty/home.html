Frequently Asked Questions

Explore our FAQ section for comprehensive answers to common queries, tailored for students.

1.

What types of internships can I find on StartIn?

StartIn offers a wide range of internships across various industries, including technology, business, healthcare, non-profit, and more. You can find internships in areas like marketing, engineering, finance, research, and many others.

2.

How much does it cost to use StartIn?

There is no cost associated with using StartIn. Entrepreneurship Cell, IIT Kharagpur is a non profit organization and the aim of this initiative is to connect talented students and companies.

3.

Can I find remote internships on StartIn?

Yes, StartIn features a variety of internship options, including remote, on-site, and hybrid positions. You can filter your search by location and work preferences to find internships that best suit your need and choose the preferred option while filling up the form as well.

4.

Is my data safe on StartIn?

Yes, we take your privacy and data security very seriously. We use industry-standard security measures to protect your personal information.
